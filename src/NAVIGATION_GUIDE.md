# How to View the Canvas Workshop Manager

## Quick Navigation Path:

1. **From the Sidebar** → Click on "Your Brand"
2. **Select an Asset** → Click on "Golden Circle Framework" (Asset #1)
3. **View Research Options** → Click on "Canvas Workshop" in the research methods section
4. The Canvas Workshop Manager will now display with all the new UX improvements

## Alternative: Direct Sidebar Navigation

The sidebar also has direct links to research options. Look for:
- Your Brand → Golden Circle Framework → Canvas Workshop

## What You'll See:

### To Buy View (Default)
- **Asset Selection Cards** - Choose between:
  - Pre-Selected Bundles (Starter, Professional, Complete)
  - Individual Asset Selection
- **Impact Preview Sidebar** - Shows which asset pages will be updated
- **Your Workshop Configuration** - Pricing breakdown and options
- **Post-Purchase Flow** - 4-step process overview
- **Enhanced Facilitator Add-on** - With bullet-point benefits

### Key New Features:
1. **Impact Preview** - Click "Preview Dashboard Changes" to see before/after
2. **Bundle Confirmation** - Warns when expanding scope
3. **Detailed Pricing** - Line-by-line breakdown with bundle savings
4. **Purchase Button** - Disabled until at least 1 asset selected
5. **Mode Switching** - Toggle between bundles and custom (preserves selection)

### Status Views:
Use the dropdown in the top-right to switch between:
- **To Buy** - Purchase and configure workshops (shows all new features)
- **In Progress** - Active workshop sessions
- **Approved** - Completed workshop results

## Currently Configured Route:
- Asset ID: `1` (Golden Circle Framework)
- Research Option: `canvas-workshop`
- Status: `approved` (you can see all states via the dropdown)
